Here's a little bit more about me.


I'm an undergraduate from University of California, San Diego with a major in Computer Science and minor in Design.

As a designer, I love to discover different perspectives and look into the purpose and intent behind a design. As a developer, I believe technology is incomplete without examining the constantly changing human-computer interaction. As a photographer, I shoot portraitures to understand others a little bit more. Even though we spend time to get to know a person, I don't think we can truly 100% understand a person, even the closest of family and friends. It can be scary, but I find it intriguing and worth exploring.
